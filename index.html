Manga recommendation

Here are few mangas/manhuas/manhwas I would like to recommend

Dark Heaven

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

  • Adult
  • Yaoi
  • Webtoons
  • Drama

Conor, a Korean-American, lives in a city where same-sex marriage has been legalized, but where racist and homophobic hate crimes are still frequent. He joins a band with the blonde hair, blue-eyed Simon and the two go on to create their own “dark heaven” in an abandoned church. Feelings develop between the pair, but light cannot dwell with dark, and their time together will soon come to an end.

Banana Fish

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

  • Action
  • Drama
  • Mystery
  • Tragedy
  • Shounen Ai

Ash Lynx, a runaway picked off the streets of New York City and raised by the infamous godfather of the mafia, Dino Golzine. Now 17 and the boss of his own gang, Ash begins investigating the mysterious "Banana Fish"—the same two words his older brother, Griffin, has muttered since his return from the Iraq War. However, his inquiries are hindered by Dino.
At Ash's bar, he is introduced to Eiji Okumura, a young and naive Japanese photographer reporting on American street gangs. Their fates intertwined shows an incredible relationship between them. Now, Ash must find a way to continue his investigation into Banana Fish, but will his history with the mafia prevent him from succeeding?

Solo Levelling

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

  • Action
  • Adventure
  • Fantasy
  • Drama

10 years ago, after “the Gate” that connected the real world with the monster world opened, some of the ordinary, everyday people received the power to hunt monsters within the Gate. They are known as "Hunters". However, not all Hunters are powerful. This is the story of Sung Jin-Woo, an E-rank Hunter, the "World's Weakest" with no skills whatsoever to display fighting in low-leveled dungeons. However, fate has something else for him, discovering a hidden dungeon during one of his hunt with the hardest difficulty where he miraculously recieves a strange power. Which one day ends up making him the strongest S-rank Hunter!

Dear Gene

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

  • Yaoi
  • Smut
  • Drama
  • Romance

While vacationing at his uncle's NYC home, Gene stumbles across an old journal. Inside, he discovers his uncle's past with a man of the same name: Gene Trevor Edwards has finally made it as a New York lawyer. His success is put in jeopardy when he loses an envelope containing confidential work documents. Thankfully, his guardian angel arrives—Gene, a young homeless man, found the letter and searched high and low to return it to him. Seeing his shabby state and unable to leave his savior in the cold, Trevor offers Gene a job as a live-in housekeeper. This is the story of a beautiful amish youth and a gay lawyer living together in the heart of New York City.

White Bood

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

  • Action
  • Fantasy
  • Romance

When vampires destroy her chance to have the normal life she’s always wanted, Hayan is forced to draw on her darkest secret to rid the world of the merciless hunters that took it over 10 years ago. With the support of a rising star in the local police force, Hayan steps up to protect her loved ones, face her past, and get her revenge.

The Fairy Bath Thief

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

  • Shounen Ai
  • Comedy
  • Drama

Hyoshin, who left home in search of freedom, found himself deep in the mountain in the middle of tiger territory. Fallen off the path, he happens on upon a bathing fema- no! male fairy. The fairies who come down to the mountain to bathe experienced a small theft and Hyoshin becomes the prime suspect. Now he's under the careful watch of San!
